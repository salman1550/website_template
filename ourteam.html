History

Sharp blades was established in 2015 by John Chester Tall tasks are cut short with our electric carving knives. Featuring a powerful motor and serrated stainless-steel blade, our electric kitchen knives take the grunt work out of carving restaurant-quality cuts. They’re perfect for cutting meats, breads or vegetables. Easy to operate, our electric carving knives can be turned on or off from a one-touch button. The ergonomic, nonslip handle makes it easy for right or left handers to maneuver with ease. They also come equipped with a long cord, allowing you to slice and serve turkey, roast beef or veggie roast, right from the table. You won’t miss a single moment. Although most electric knives come with the same style of blade, some will add wider or thinner blades for different tasks. If you plan to do a lot of cutting and carving, keep this in mind and look for one that has the versatility you need.
